<p><i>App2Proxy</i> lets you redirect the traffic of selected apps through a local proxy using <code>iptables</code>. Due to the use of <code>iptables</code>, the app requires root access.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Selective traffic redirection: choose specific applications for proxying</li><li>Automatic restoration of iptables rules after reboot</li><li>Support for system apps with advanced mode</li><li>DNS management: redirect DNS requests to port 10853</li><li>Material 3 design with dark theme and AMOLED support</li><li>Autorun with support for Android 15+ and various manufacturers</li><li>Autoload diagnostics to troubleshoot problems</li><li>Rule manager to view and manage active rules</li></ul>
